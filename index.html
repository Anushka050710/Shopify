Our Story

Born from a love of slow living

Lumo began in a small kitchen in 2019 — a single pot of soy wax, a handful of botanicals, and a belief that the right scent can transform any space into a sanctuary.

Every candle we make is still poured by hand, in small batches, using only natural ingredients. No shortcuts. No fillers. Just honest craft and beautiful fragrance.
